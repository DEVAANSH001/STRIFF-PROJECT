Introduction to Sudoku Solver

Sudoku is a logic-based combinatorial number-placement puzzle, where the objective is to fill a 9x9 grid with digits from 1 to 9, so that each column, each row, and each of the nine 3x3 subgrids (also known as blocks or regions) contain all of the digits from 1 to 9 without repetition.
